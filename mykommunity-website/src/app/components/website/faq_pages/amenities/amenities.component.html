Amenities and its benefits

For smooth running of society facilities we have online booking of all amenities like clubhouse , sport arena , dormitory and other facilities through mobile app . We can make payment to those services through our payment gateway app.

Can I book amenities through MyKommunity ?

    You need to:
  • Click on the 'Community' tab on the app home screen
  • Click on 'Amenities'
  • Click on 'Book Amenity' at the bottom of the app screen
  • Select the Amenity, date and time slot
  • Click on 'Book Now'

Can I Pay online to the services ?

    You need to:  
  • Click on the 'Community' tab on the app home screen
  • Click on ' Amenities '
  • Click on 'Book Amenity' at the bottom of the app screen
  • Select the Amenity, date and time slot
  • Click on 'Proceed to Payment'
  • Select the Payment type
  • Click on 'Book Now'
